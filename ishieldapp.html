IShield App

The iShield App is your gateway to earning money through task completion. Our platform offers a unique opportunity to earn rewards by completing simple tasks, investing a minimal amount, and engaging with a user-friendly interface designed to make earning easy and enjoyable.

Key Features

  • Daily Rewards: Earn a guaranteed reward of 10 rupees every day just for logging in and completing daily tasks.
  • Weekly and Monthly Tasks: Participate in a variety of weekly and monthly tasks, each offering substantial rewards, including up to 250 rupees for weekly tasks and 950 rupees for monthly tasks.
  • Investment Opportunities: Make a one-time investment of 250 rupees to unlock the full potential of the app and start earning from task completions.
  • Flexible Task Completion: Tasks are revealed weekly, allowing you to manage your time and complete tasks at your convenience. Stay engaged to avoid disqualification.
  • Secure Withdrawals: Withdraw your earnings after one year of consistent activity. Our secure system ensures that your funds are protected and accessible.


How It Works

  1. Sign Up: Create an account on the iShield App and complete the registration process.
  2. Invest: Make a one-time investment of 250 rupees to unlock full access to the app’s features.
  3. Complete Tasks: Engage with daily, weekly, and monthly tasks to earn rewards. Stay active to maximize your earnings.
  4. Withdraw Earnings: After one year, you can request a withdrawal of your accumulated earnings. Our system ensures secure and timely transactions.
