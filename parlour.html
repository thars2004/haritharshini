Bruntha Beauty Parlour Management System

Manage your beauty parlour efficiently with our comprehensive management system.

System Overview



Dashboard View: Centralized overview of appointments, services, and customer details.



Appointment Booking: Easy interface to book and manage appointments.



Invoice View: Generate and view invoices for customer appointments.







Admin Management



Add Service: Easily add and update services offered at the parlour.



Appointment Status: Monitor and update the status of customer appointments.



Data Reports: Access detailed reports on services, appointments, and customer interactions.



System Features

The Bruntha Beauty Parlour Management System is designed to streamline the management of appointments, services, and customer relations. Users can easily book appointments, view their status, and access invoices. Admins have powerful tools to manage services, track appointments, and generate comprehensive reports.

This system is built using PHP, MySQL, and JavaScript, offering a secure and user-friendly experience for both customers and administrators.
